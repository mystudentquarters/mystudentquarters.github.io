What is My Student Quarters?

My Student Quarters is your one-stop shop (well, website) for everything related to off-campus student housing. Our site guides you from finding your next apartment to hiring a cleaning service upon move-out.

We work with landlords and realtors to post local units with professional pictures, lease information, and even a map that shows you the unit's distance to your university.

With 50+ housing filters, you are able to search efficiently for housing that meets your living style, including variable lease lengths. Once you found your spot, our platform connects you with the verified landlord, creates your housing dashboard for all the information related to your current unit and provides resources for every situation you may find yourself in as a student.

This housing dashboard will be where you can search for roommates, get information on safe and easy methods to pay your landlord or contact us with questions related to your housing. My Student Quarters knows what it's like to be a student, because we’re students too.

There are a lot of exciting, stressful challenges associated with being a student, but finding and maintaining your housing no longer has to be one of them.

We are student housing that is made for students, by students. We know what it is like to be a student and we live in the same environment as our customers. We are the customer.

Our company keeps students involved by offering an ambassador program, full and part-time paid positions, and internships.

We put the power in our people!

What is it That Provides Value to Our Service?

Two words - Convenience and Security!

All members, landlords and students alike, are verified by our platform before being able to access the full MSQ website. We collaborated with our friends at Checkr to confirm accurate, reliable background screens are completed for all members of the MSQ community. We also serve as an excellent resource for how you can pay rent to your landlord. Rental horror stories just aren’t really our look.

Furthermore, MSQ offers the opportunity for students and landlords to be reviewed by one another after a lease ends. This gives community members the ability to make more educated decisions about future housing arrangements before a contract is signed.
